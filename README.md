**SQL-based analytics project that aggregates and ranks account and email activity across countries**.

_Key features_:

-  CTEs for structured analysis:

-  Account metrics (verification, unsubscribe status, send intervals)

-  Email metrics (sent, opened, visited messages)

-  Unified metrics combining account + email data

_Aggregations_: totals by country, grouped by date and account/email activity

_Ranking logic_: dense rank applied to top countries by account count and email activity

_Final output_: top 10 countries by account and email metrics, with daily breakdowns

_Visualization_:

Integrated with Looker Studio dashboard for interactive exploration
